Welcome to Your Getaway

Welcome to 79th on Main, where barn charm meets boutique comfort. This lovingly restored 1920s barn has been transformed into your personal countryside retreat, complete with modern amenities and timeless character.

Our Story

Hi! We're Jason and Kristen, Penn State grads from State College, Pennsylvania who first stayed here as guests in 2023. We loved the experience so much that when the opportunity arose to purchase the property, we jumped at the chance to share this special place with others.

We understand the need to step away from the everyday hustle—to find a place where you can truly unwind. That's what this barn offers: a luxury retreat where morning coffee tastes better on the porch, afternoons are meant for exploring wineries, and evenings are for gathering around the fire pit.

As frequent visitors to the Finger Lakes (often with our 100-pound Bernedoodle, Doug), we've curated this guide with our favorite local spots and hidden gems. We want your stay to be as relaxing and memorable as possible. Whether you're here for the wine trails, the waterfalls, or simply the peace and quiet, we hope this barn becomes your perfect escape from the ordinary.

This guidebook contains everything you need for a comfortable stay. Take a moment to settle in, pour yourself something delicious from the kitchen, and let the tranquility of the countryside wash over you.

We're delighted to have you as our guest. Enjoy your stay!

Kitchen & Appliances

Coffee Station

Start your morning right with our fully stocked coffee bar:

  • Moccamaster Drip Coffee: Add filter to basket, measure coffee (7g per cup), fill water reservoir to desired level, flip the power switch. Coffee brews in 4-6 minutes at the perfect 200°F
  • Pour Over: Place filter in dripper, rinse with hot water, add medium-fine grounds (15g per 250ml), bloom with small amount of water for 30 seconds, then pour remaining water in slow circles
  • French Press: Add coarse grounds (3 tbsp), pour hot water, steep 4 minutes, press slowly

Outdoor Spaces

Propane Fire Pit

Enjoy cozy evenings around our propane fire pit! Simply turn the gas knob to ignite (lighter may be needed for first ignition). Adjust flame height with the control knob. Please turn off completely before heading inside for the night.

Grill

  • Propane tank valve on top of tank (turn counterclockwise)
  • Push and turn burner knobs to ignite
  • Grill tools in drawer below
  • Extra propane tank in shed if needed

Check-out Process

We hope you've enjoyed your stay! Check-out is at 11:00 AM and is simple:

  • Take any trash to the bins at the end of the driveway
  • Start the dishwasher if you've used dishes (detergent pods under kitchen sink)
  • Leave used towels in the bathroom
  • Close and lock all windows
  • Turn off all lights
  • Turn off all window AC units
  • Turn off propane to grill and fire pit
  • Set thermostat to 62°F (October-April)
  • Double-check for personal items (chargers, toiletries, etc.)
  • Lock the door and ensure it latches

That's it! No need to strip beds or deep clean - we'll take care of the rest.

Late Check-out: We consider late check-out requests on a case-by-case basis. Please message us in the app by 9 AM on your departure day and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Before You Go: We'd love to hear about your stay! Please consider leaving us a review on Airbnb - your feedback helps us continue to improve and helps other travelers discover this special place.
