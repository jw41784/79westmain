Welcome to 79th on Main, where barn charm meets boutique comfort. This lovingly restored 1920s barn has been transformed into your personal countryside retreat, complete with modern amenities and timeless character.

Our Story

Sometimes, the best love stories begin with a simple getaway. We're Jason and Kristen, and in 2023, we arrived here as guests, booking this very barn for what we thought would be just another weekend escape from State College. But something magical happened the moment we stepped through these doors.

The morning light filtering through original barn windows, the peaceful evenings on the porch, the way time seemed to slow down‚Äîwe didn't just stay here, we fell completely in love. When fate presented the opportunity to purchase this special place, we knew it was meant to be. Now, with our 100-pound Bernedoodle Doug by our side, we're the grateful stewards of this slice of Finger Lakes paradise.

As Penn State graduates who've explored countless corners of this region, we can honestly say there's nowhere quite like Trumansburg. Multiple times a year, we're drawn back to these rolling hills, these pristine lakes, these welcoming small towns. We hope that during your stay, you'll discover what we did‚Äîthat some places don't just offer a bed for the night; they offer a feeling of coming home.

This guidebook contains everything you need for a comfortable stay. Take a moment to settle in, pour yourself something delicious from the kitchen, and let the tranquility of the countryside wash over you.

We're delighted to have you as our guest. May your time here be filled with rest, renewal, and the same magic that captured our hearts.

Fireplace

Gas Fireplace Instructions:

  1. Ensure flue is open (lever on right side)
  2. Turn gas valve to "ON" position
  3. Press ignitor button while turning control knob
  4. Hold for 30 seconds until pilot lights
  5. Use remote control for flame height
