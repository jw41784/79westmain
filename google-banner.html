How to Save as Google Maps Banner

  1. On Mac: Press Cmd + Shift + 4, then press Space, click on the banner above
  2. On Windows: Press Win + Shift + S, select the banner area
  3. Alternative: Right-click the banner → "Inspect" → Right-click the <div class="banner-container"> → "Capture node screenshot"
  4. The banner is sized at 1080x608px (16:9 ratio) - perfect for Google Maps cover photos
  5. Upload the saved image to your Google Business Profile as your cover photo
